🎯 Performance Presets

One-tap tuning to hold ~30 FPS for your device's RAM. Higher tiers unlock more visual quality while staying at 30 FPS; PC is the only tier allowed to go beyond 30 FPS.

Presets set resolution scale, FPS cap, textures, shadows, AA, atmosphere/lighting, 3D tile quality, cache and preload radius all at once. You can still fine-tune any value below afterward.
